# First Three Quizzes

### Working title

Traffic Readiness Diagnostic

### Core promise

Check whether the business system is ready to receive paid traffic without wasting budget.

### Main segments

- Site is not ready for traffic
- Offer is weak
- Traffic exists but leads are poor
- Leads exist but sales are weak
- AI qualification is needed
- Full ClientFlow System is needed

### Offer routes

- ClientFlow Audit
- Delaem Site
- AI Lead Qualification
- Full ClientFlow AI System

### Visual direction

Founder control room. Dark premium. AI sales architecture. CRM, traffic, qualification and analytics nodes.
