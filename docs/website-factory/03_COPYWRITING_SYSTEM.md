# 03_COPYWRITING_SYSTEM — Website Factory

## Purpose
Create conversion-first website copy that explains value quickly, qualifies the right visitor, and moves them toward a commercial action.

## Core Principle
Copy is not decoration. Copy is the operating system of the page.

Every block must answer one of four questions:

1. Why should I care?
2. Why should I trust this?
3. What exactly do I get?
4. What should I do next?

## Hero Formula

### Kicker
Who / role / context.

### H1
The main outcome in one sharp promise.

### Subheadline
Who it is for, what makes the approach different, and why it matters.

### Primary CTA
The strongest commercial next step.

### Secondary CTA
A softer route for colder visitors.

### Proof Row
4–5 compact trust or system markers.

## Section Copy Formula

For every section:

1. Kicker — context marker.
2. H2 — one clear idea.
3. Body — short explanation.
4. Bullets/cards — proof, mechanics, or steps.
5. CTA — next action or micro-action.

## Strong Copy Patterns

### Problem
Show the cost of the current broken system.

### Method
Explain the operating logic, not abstract expertise.

### Offer
Break the product into concrete parts.

### Proof
Use artifacts, process, screenshots, numbers, before/after, or founder authority.

### CTA
Use action language: разобрать, собрать, проверить, обсудить, получить.

## Anti-Patterns

Avoid:

- индивидуальный подход;
- команда профессионалов;
- лучшие решения;
- полный спектр услуг;
- качественно и в срок;
- повысим продажи without mechanism;
- generic agency claims;
- long hero paragraphs;
- vague transformations;
- fake urgency.

## CTA System

### Hot CTA
Used when the visitor is ready to talk.
Examples: Обсудить проект, Разбор системы, Собрать контур.

### Warm CTA
Used when the visitor needs diagnosis.
Examples: Проверить маршрут, Понять точки потерь.

### Cold CTA
Used when the visitor wants to observe first.
Examples: Telegram-канал, Посмотреть подход.

## Tone
Direct, calm, precise, architectural.

No hype. No fluff. No fake guarantees.

## Quality Gate
Copy is accepted only if:

- the offer is clear in 3 seconds;
- each section has one job;
- the CTA is obvious;
- claims have mechanics or proof;
- the visitor can understand the next step without explanation.
