# i18n Foundation

The site is prepared for bilingual growth without duplicating the full Russian homepage yet.

## Current status

- Russian homepage remains the primary site.
- `/en` exists as a dedicated English foundation page.
- The English route is not a full translation of every Russian section.
- The English route uses the same strategic positioning and a compact, serious structure.

## Translation direction

The English message keeps the original meaning, not a literal word-for-word translation.

Primary positioning:

- RU: `Собираю бизнесу управляемую систему привлечения клиентов`
- EN: `I build controlled client acquisition systems for businesses`

## What is already localized

- `/en` metadata
- English hero copy
- English ClientFlow framing
- English loss map
- English service ladder
- English process outline
- English contact foundation

## What remains for full bilingualization

- English homepage parity for all Russian sections
- English legal pages
- Locale-aware content dictionaries
- Localized cases/articles entries
- A formal language switch policy for header/footer placement
- Final canonical/hreflang review after production domain connection

## Future content approach

- Keep locale in content models (`ru` / `en`)
- Add separate locale entries instead of machine-translating the Russian source
- Move shared text into structured dictionaries or content files only when a second locale becomes fully maintained

## Notes

- No CMS, database, or external localization service is connected yet.
- No English legal pages were created in this pass.
- The `/en` route links back to the Russian contact flow instead of duplicating the lead form.
