## Do Not Do

- Do not distort the face.
- Do not replace the identity.
- Do not make the face AI-plastic.
- Do not over-smooth skin.
- Do not change the founder into a generic stock-person look.
- Do not make the portrait a simple photo card.
- Do not cover the face/body with UI panels.
- Do not expose raw source images in Git.
- Do not commit `docs/portrait-source/` source files unless explicitly approved.
- Do not move or rename source files without approval.

## Desired Portrait Direction

- Cinematic founder authority.
- Integrated with the control-room atmosphere.
- Natural skin and believable face quality.
- Premium dark lighting with cool blue rim/depth.
- Subtle masks and scene-level framing, not a rectangular card.
- Human anchor first; ClientFlow system cues second.
